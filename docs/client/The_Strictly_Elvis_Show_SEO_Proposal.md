## Findings

The following issues were identified during the scan, organized by potential impact on 
your search rankings and local visibility.

### 🔴 Title Tag — High Impact

**Finding:** Title is too long (65 chars; aim for 50–60)


### 🔴 NAP Consistency (Website) — High Impact

**Finding:** No street address detected — displaying your address consistently is critical for local search rankings (NAP consistency)


### 🔴 Google Business Profile — High Impact

**Finding:** Phone number mismatch — website shows a different number than the Google Business Profile ((262) 293-9805). Inconsistent NAP data confuses search engines and can suppress rankings


### 🟡 Google Business Profile — Medium Impact

**Finding:** Only 7 Google review(s) — businesses with 10+ reviews see significantly better local pack visibility

**Finding:** No business description on the Google Business Profile — a well-written description with relevant keywords improves both rankings and conversions


### 🟡 Heading Structure — Medium Impact

**Finding:** Multiple H1 tags found (2) — use only one per page


### 🟡 Canonical Tags — Medium Impact

**Finding:** No canonical tag found — without it, Google may index duplicate versions of your pages and split your ranking authority


### 🟡 Image Optimization — Medium Impact

**Finding:** 6 images missing alt text — alt text helps search engines understand your images

## Suggested Scope of Work

Based on the findings above, here is a summary of recommended improvements:

- Rewrite and optimize page title tags with geographic keywords
- Ensure Name, Address, and Phone appear consistently site-wide
- Restructure page headings for proper H1/H2 hierarchy
- Add descriptive alt text to all images
- Add canonical tags to prevent duplicate content issues
- Audit and optimize Google Business Profile listing
- Write keyword-rich Google Business Profile description
- Implement a review generation strategy for Google
