Entertainment Options

Our Shows

Three unique experiences, each tailored to make your event unforgettable.

The Strictly Elvis Show

Your chance to have your very own personalized Elvis concert. Whether it is for 2 people or 1,000, you are sure to enjoy the show.

This interactive performance features scarf handouts to audience members and customizable song requests spanning Elvis hits from the 1950s through the 1970s.

All shows can be tailored to your individual needs, and requests for songs to be performed are always welcomed.

The Classic Hits Show

A multi-artist tribute show featuring the greatest hits from legendary performers including Elvis Presley, Frank Sinatra, Dean Martin, Andy Williams, Neil Diamond, The Monkees, and Glen Campbell.

Covering songs from the 1950s through the 1990s, this show delivers a wide range of classic entertainment perfect for any audience.

The King & Diamonds

An Elvis Presley and Neil Diamond tribute show featuring Alan Graveen as Elvis and Eric Ebert as Neil Diamond.

Eric has performed with Alan for 6 years and brings over 13 years of experience as a Neil Diamond tribute artist. Together they deliver a powerhouse duo show.
