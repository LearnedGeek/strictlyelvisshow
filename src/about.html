About Alan Graveen

Over 20 years performing as Wisconsin's favorite Elvis tribute artist

Meet Alan

Alan Graveen has been performing as an Elvis Tribute Artist for over 20 years and working as a mobile DJ since 2000. With more than 5,000 shows under his belt, he brings unmatched experience and energy to every performance.

In 2007, he established Magic Moments DJ as a "3-in-1 entertainment solution" for various events, then including karaoke services alongside his Elvis tribute and DJ offerings. Requests soon came in for mainly "Strictly Elvis" shows, which, for many years now, has been Alan's primary focus.

Alan regularly performs at senior living communities, retirement homes, and assisted living facilities throughout Wisconsin, bringing joy and nostalgia to audiences who grew up with Elvis's music.

Based in Lannon, Wisconsin, Alan serves communities throughout southeastern Wisconsin, traveling up to 100 miles for the right event.
